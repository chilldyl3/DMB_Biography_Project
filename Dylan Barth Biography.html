Biography Timeline

Early Life

Born on August 19th, 1998 in Louisville, Kentucky. Attended Brandeis Elementary for grades K-5 developing a love for many activities including sports, music, chess. These activities would later become a large part of Dylan's Life. One sport in particular, (Soccer) would later become his favorite and he would continue playing it through college. Dylan also started playing several musical instruments during this time.

Middle Years

After attending Brandeis Elementary, Dylan attended Noe Middle School and later duPont Manual High School.. By middle school Dylan narrowed down some of the activities to focus on what he enjoyed the most. In the middle school days Dylan focused primarily on Soccer, Baseball, Basketball, and clarinet. He received honors in all of these seen below.

Middle School Honors

  • 2x City Champions in Basketball
  • 3x All State Band for clarinet
  • 2x State Soccer Champions
  • 3x State and Regional Champions in Baseball

College and Beyond

After graduating from duPont Manual in 2017 Dylan attended Centre College in Danville, Kentucky. He studied hard while also playing for CMS He earned a B.S. in Economics and Financial Economics and helped lead CMS to four conference Championships. His Junior year, in 2019 he scored the equalizing goal in the Elite Eight with less than minute left on the clock. The goal forced overtime in which CMS came out victorious and secured the program's first Final Four berth.

Centre Men's Soccer

CMS started as a club sport in 1973 at Centre College. At the time soccer was considered a club sport and not a sanction sport by colleges and universities. In 2019, CMS became the lowest rated seed to reach a Final Four.
